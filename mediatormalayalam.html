Malayalam Alphabets

! The Malayalam alphabet, derived from the Brahmi script, consists of 52 letters. It includes 15 vowel letters and 37 consonant letters. The script is unique for its ability to represent complex consonant clusters.

Malayalam Phrases

Malayalam phrases are a window to Kerala's vibrant culture, from the warm greeting "Namaskaram" to the affectionate "Njan ninne premikkunnu" (I love you). "Onnu koodi" (Come here) and "Vayya" (Hey) are common informal phrases. Embracing these phrases enriches your connection with Malayalam-speaking communities.
