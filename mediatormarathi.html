Marathi Alphabets

Marathi alphabets are the script used to write the Marathi language, a language spoken mainly in the Indian state of Maharashtra. The script is known as the Devanagari script and consists of 36 letters. Each letter represents a consonant sound, and there are also diacritics for vowel sounds.

Marathi Phrases

Marathi phrases reflect the vibrant culture of Maharashtra, India. From greetings to everyday expressions, they embody the warmth and diversity of the Marathi language. Learning these phrases can help you connect with Marathi-speaking people and explore the beauty of the language.
